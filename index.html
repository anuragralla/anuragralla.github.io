About Me

I am Anurag, a grad student at the University of Florida. I completeled my undergrad from SRM Institute of Science and Technology,India majoring in Software Engineering. I am a Machine Learning enthusiast and believe that we are the midst of a revolution with Machine Learning @ core of it. I believe in using my tech skills to make the world a better place to live in as exhibited when I interned with one of India's largest companies Reliance Jio in their AI/ML Center of Excellence. During this time I helped build a dashboard to help out farmers.

I am problem-solver that believes in boiling the problem down to its fundamentals and then working up towards the problem. I mainly code in Python. adding C++, Java when required.
